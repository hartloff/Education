== Sally's Vacation

Sally, after her hard work and contributions to the company, accumulated a lot of paid vacation days! She thinks it’s a good time take a break from work and travel somewhere cool before she conquers another one of her company’s big problems.





Sally made a good chunk of change, so money is no object, but she only has a limited amount of time. She wants to find the perfect vacation spot to spend her first vacation since starting her job!





She thought the music collections software she wrote a while back worked really well! The amount of feedback she got from her co-workers were phenomenal, so she modified the old code a little and repurposed it.





She has to fill out a ton of paperwork to get her vacation time, so she’s hoping you can help her with creating a user-friendly application using her new API. Can you help Sally out with her idea?
