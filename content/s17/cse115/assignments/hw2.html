== Sally's new job

Thanks to your help, Sally was able to find the perfect balance in her life throughout her 4 years in college. She tested many different scenarios before finding a lifestyle that she can enjoy while leaving enough time to do well in her classes.





By living this lifestyle Sally was able to find her dream job at a major investing firm. Sally is excited to start her new job and start cashing those big paychecks! While transitioning to the working world Sally plans to make minor adjustments to her lifestyle, but she's confident that she doesn't have to make any sacrifices in her personal life.





Just as Sally is getting comfortable in her new position, her boss asks her to review every trade made at the firm throughout 2016 for three major stocks in the tech sector (AAPL, GOOG, and MSFT) and compute the firms return on investment (ROI) from trading these stocks. While Sally is excited to find this ROI, she is not looking forward to processing the 1000+ trades that have been made in 2016. Computing the return on all these trades will require significant overtime which will disrupt the balanced lifestyle Sally has been enjoying. Since Sally is a salaried employee this also means she'll receive the same pay even though she'll be working more hours.





Knowing that her boss will likely have similar tasks for her in the future, Sally decides she wants software that will automate this process. Sally hasn't studied much programming, but she knows investing and wrote an outline of a java program that will process the data. She converted all the trades to csv files separated by trader, assembled all the historic prices for the relevant stocks, and wrote some testing code. She also wrote descriptions of everything that the software needs to do, but she's not sure how to complete the methods described. If you help Sally with this software, she will help you organize your finances and begin investing in your future.

Can you help Sally finish this program?
