== Sally

Sally is a college freshman adjusting to her new environment. Sally enjoys eating at restaurants with her friends, hanging out over a few drinks (juice of course), and getting plenty of sleep. To pay for this, Sally works part-time as a server, but she's not sure if there's enough time to live this same lifestyle in college. Between classes, sleep, and relaxing, will she be able to work enough hours to pay for her food and drink each week? Or will Sally have homemade rice and water every day?





Sally plans to enjoy more than just the next 4 years and will learn how to start a career that will fund a more luxurious lifestyle after graduation. During orientation she heard the recommended time she should spend on her courses and she plans to budget this much time for her studies.





Sally is determined to balance all aspects of her life and make the next 4 years as enjoyable as possible without sacrificing her future. With only 24 hours each day, she's not sure if she'll have enough time to do everything.

Your task is to write a program where Sally can test different lifestyle choices and determine how well she can live during her college years which achieving her short-term and long-term goals.
