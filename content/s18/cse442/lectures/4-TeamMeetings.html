--- title: Team Meetings short_title: meetings next_content_short: jury previous_content_short: vc --- == Protocol
  • Meet for at least 1 full hour per week in Baldy 19 at your weekly meeting time
  • Submit notice of absence at least 24 hours prior to the meeting time with a valid reason
  • Push meeting minutes to your project repository

Minutes

After each meeting, in a directory names "minutes" push a document containing the following information:

  • Meeting date and time
  • Meeting attendance. Any team member who are missing must be marked as either "unexcused absence" or "excused absence" along with a valid reason for the absence with notification to the team more than 24 hours prior to the meeting
  • Brief summary of the meeting discussion
  • List explicitly and in detail what each individual team member accomplished since the last meeting
  • List explicitly and in detail what each individual team member will accomplish before the next meeting. All team members must have at least 1 task listed here and each task must have a GitHub issue associated with it in the project repository

Staff Visits

On occasion the course staff may visit your team meeting if there is something we'd like to discuss with you. You must be able to accommodate this in your meeting schedule. A staff member may visit if there are any issues with the project, or if we can offer any specialized help to improve your project. Your team may also request a visit if you want help with anything pertaining to your project.
