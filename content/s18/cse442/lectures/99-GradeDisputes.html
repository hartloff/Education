--- title: Grade Disputes short_title: disputes next_content_short: makeup previous_content_short: bugs --- == Grade Disputes

If you believe there has been a mistake in your grade on a particular assignment you may dispute your grade by following this protocol. This is not an invitation to complain about a grade you earned, but it provides a means to correct any errors that may have occurred during the grading process.

== Protocol
  • Fill out the form within 1 week of the release of the disputed grade

Dispute Form

Form: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSevqskYgm_8svOfVpneWDfxYSiLXOR9ySMX-o_FMozWtUVznw/viewform?usp=sf_link

If you have a reasonable belief that there was an error made while determining your grade you can use this form to bring the error to the attention of the course staff. A grade dispute must be submitted within 1 week of the time the grade being disputed was released.
