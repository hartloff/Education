== Lecture

Many lectures will involve interactive activities. These activities are designed to help you understand and apply various aspects of software engineering. In addition, we will explore software engineering as a whole and discuss it's role in society.

These activities will be self-contained in lecture and you must attend lecture to earn credit for them.

== Grading

Each activity is graded on a binary scale. You either completed the activity, or you didn't. The effect on your final grade will be determined by the number of activities you didn't complete.

Incomplete Activities Grade Change
0 +1
1-3 0
4-5 -1
6-7 -2
8+ -3

*If the response rate on the course evaluations is at least 98%, all cutoff values for incomplete activities will be increased by 1. This would mean that missing 0-1 activities would result in +1, miss 2-4 for 0, etc.

== Make Up

For each activity there will be a make up opportunity for any student who attended lecture and attempted the activity, but was unable to successfully complete it. No make ups will be given to student who either missed lecture, or were present but did not make an honest effort to complete the activity.

Make ups will also be available for anyone with an excused absence. Excused absences must be supported with official documentation (doctor's note, police report, athletic schedule). No undocumented excuses will be entertained.

The intent of these activities is to further your understanding of software engineering, not cause stress about completing each activity. Take your time, relax, and use the make up opportunities if needed.
