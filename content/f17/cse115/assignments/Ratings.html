== Background

Rating systems are all around. Whether we are shopping online, looking for a place to eat, or deciding which movie to see most of us will look up some form ratings or reviews before we decide how to spend our hard earned cash. In this assignment we will explore different aspect of ratings and reviews and eventual build our own system where we can share our [music] preferences.
